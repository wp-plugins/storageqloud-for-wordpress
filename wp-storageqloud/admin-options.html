This plugin allows you to easily upload, retrieve, and link to files stored on GreenQloud's Truly Greenâ„¢ StorageQloud from within WordPress. StorageQloud is a cheap, carbon neutral and cost effective way to scale your site to easily handle large spikes in traffic (such as from Digg) without having to go through the expense of setting up the infrastructure for a content delivery network. StorageQloud is API compatible with AWS S3.

StorageQloud Setup: If you don't have a StorageQloud account yet go Sign up on GreenQloud's website >

Plugin Installation and Usage: Just follow the onscreen prompts to link this plugin to your StorageQloud account.
